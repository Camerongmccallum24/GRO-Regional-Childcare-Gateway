ACECQA DAMA Skills Assessment Guide

This guide provides comprehensive information about the Designated Area Migration Agreement (DAMA) skills assessment process for childcare occupations. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a successful application.

Important Notice: All applications must be submitted through ACECQA's online portal. Migration agents and applicants MUST use their own email addresses when registering. ACECQA does not fast track applications unless there are exceptional circumstances.

Step 1: Eligibility Check

You are eligible if your occupation is one of:

  • Child Care Worker
  • Family Day Care Worker
  • Preschool Aide
  • Out of School Hours Care Worker

Child Care Worker Regions:

  • (East) Kimberley Region in Western Australia
  • Far North Queensland
  • Goldfields Western Australia
  • Great South Coast region of Victoria
  • Northern Territory
  • Orana New South Wales
  • Pilbara Region in Western Australia
  • South Australia Regional
  • South West Region of Western Australia
  • Townsville

Other Occupations:

Family Day Care Worker: Goldfields Western Australia

Preschool Aide: (East) Kimberley Region in Western Australia

Out of School Hours Care Worker: Far North Queensland, Pilbara Region in Western Australia

Do NOT Apply If:

  • You have not completed your qualification
  • You do not meet the eligibility criteria
  • You already hold a valid skills assessment
  • You require a migration skills assessment for Child Care Centre Manager or Child Care Worker (group leaders only)
  • You require a provisional skills assessment
  • You do not have sufficient post-qualification employment experience
  • You require assessment for an occupation where ACECQA is not the Assessing Authority

Required Documentation

Standard Documentation:

  • Valid passport (identity/biometric pages)
  • Evidence of change of name (if applicable)
  • Evidence of ACECQA approved certificate III or higher
  • Official qualification certificates and transcripts
  • Employment contract and position description
  • Service registration/regulation evidence
  • Completed employer reference template
  • Completed employment experience template
  • DAMA Skills Assessment Declaration Form

Additional NQF Assessment Requirements:

  • Certified color copies of all documents
  • English language proficiency evidence
  • NQF Applicant Declaration Form
  • Translated documents (if applicable)

Application Process

Document Preparation:

  • High resolution color copies
  • Clearly visible and legible
  • Properly certified (if required)
  • Centered and complete
  • Acceptable file formats (.PDF, .JPEG, .PNG)
  • Appropriately titled
  • Correctly collated

Application Timeline:

  • Application time: ~40 minutes
  • Initial response: Within 10 working days
  • Assessment period: 60 days (once all documents received)

Important Notes:

  • Payment required for submission
  • No refunds for unsuccessful applications
  • False information is a serious offense
  • Do not use cloud storage links

Assessment Outcome

Successful Outcome:

You can submit your DAMA skills assessment as part of your visa application. Note: A successful assessment does not guarantee visa approval.

Unsuccessful Outcome:

You will receive detailed reasons for the decision. You may request a review if you have additional evidence or believe your evidence wasn't adequately considered.
